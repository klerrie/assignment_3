TERMINATION AND SEPARATION POLICIES

Clear policies ensure fair and consistent termination procedures.

VOLUNTARY TERMINATION
- Resignation notice: Minimum 2 weeks required
- Exit interview: Conducted by HR within final week
- Final paycheck: Within 3 business days of last day
- Benefits continuation: COBRA information provided
- Return of property: Equipment and access cards

INVOLUNTARY TERMINATION
- Performance-based: After improvement plan failure
- Policy violations: Immediate termination possible
- Layoffs: Business necessity, severance provided
- Documentation: All terminations documented
- Final pay: According to state law requirements

SEVERANCE PACKAGE
- Eligibility: Determined by role and tenure
- Standard package: 2 weeks per year of service
- Minimum: 4 weeks for eligible employees
- Benefits: Health insurance continuation support
- Outplacement: Career counseling services

EXIT PROCESS
- Access revocation: Same day for terminations
- Equipment return: Laptop, phone, badges
- Knowledge transfer: Documented handoff
- Final expenses: Reimbursement processed
- References: Policy on providing references

NON-COMPETE AND CONFIDENTIALITY
- Non-compete: 12 months for certain roles
- Confidentiality: Permanent obligation
- Non-solicitation: 24 months for clients and employees
- Trade secrets: Protected indefinitely
- Return of materials: All company property

REHIRE POLICY
- Eligibility: Depends on termination reason
- Performance terminations: Case-by-case review
- Layoffs: Eligible for rehire
- Process: Standard application required
- Benefits: Previous service may count toward vesting
