EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - COMPANY POLICIES

Welcome to our company! This handbook outlines important policies and procedures.

WORK SCHEDULE
- Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Flexible work arrangements available upon manager approval
- Remote work policy allows up to 3 days per week from home
- Overtime must be pre-approved by department head

LEAVE POLICIES
- Annual leave: 20 days per year for full-time employees
- Sick leave: 10 days per year, can be carried over up to 5 days
- Personal leave: 3 days per year for personal matters
- Maternity leave: 12 weeks paid leave
- Paternity leave: 2 weeks paid leave
- Bereavement leave: 5 days for immediate family

BENEFITS
- Health insurance coverage starts on first day of employment
- Dental and vision insurance available
- 401(k) matching up to 6% of salary
- Life insurance coverage equal to annual salary
- Professional development budget: $2,000 per year

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
- Annual performance reviews conducted in December
- Quarterly check-ins with managers
- Goal setting and career development planning
- Performance improvement plans available when needed

CODE OF CONDUCT
- Respectful workplace environment required
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Confidentiality of company information mandatory
- Conflict of interest disclosure required

DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS
- Verbal warning for first minor offense
- Written warning for repeated issues
- Final warning before termination
- Immediate termination for serious violations
