REMOTE WORK AND FLEXIBLE ARRANGEMENTS

Our flexible work policy supports work-life balance and productivity.

REMOTE WORK ELIGIBILITY
- Job function: Must be suitable for remote work
- Performance: Must meet or exceed expectations
- Tenure: Minimum 6 months with company
- Manager approval: Required for all arrangements
- Equipment: Company provides necessary hardware

REMOTE WORK SCHEDULE
- Standard: Up to 3 days per week remote
- Full remote: Available for approved roles
- Hybrid model: Mix of office and remote days
- Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM must be available
- Time zone: Must align with team location

HOME OFFICE SETUP
- Workspace: Dedicated, quiet space required
- Internet: Minimum 25 Mbps download speed
- Equipment: Laptop, monitor, keyboard provided
- Furniture: Ergonomic assessment available
- Security: VPN and security software required

COMMUNICATION EXPECTATIONS
- Response time: Within 2 hours during work hours
- Video calls: Camera on for team meetings
- Availability: Online status visible to team
- Collaboration tools: Slack, Teams, Zoom usage
- Office visits: Minimum 1 day per month for hybrid

PRODUCTIVITY AND PERFORMANCE
- Metrics: Same standards as office employees
- Check-ins: Regular meetings with manager
- Deliverables: Clear expectations and deadlines
- Time tracking: Required for hourly employees
- Performance reviews: Same process as office staff

POLICY CHANGES
- Review period: Annual assessment of arrangements
- Revocation: Possible if performance declines
- Modification: Can be adjusted with notice
- Compliance: Must follow all company policies
- Documentation: Agreement signed and filed
