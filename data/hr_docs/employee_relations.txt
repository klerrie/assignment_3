EMPLOYEE RELATIONS AND SUPPORT

We maintain positive employee relations through clear policies and support systems.

GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
- Informal resolution: Discuss with direct manager first
- Formal complaint: Submit to HR within 30 days
- Investigation process: Conducted by HR team
- Resolution timeline: 10 business days for response
- Appeal process: Available if unsatisfied with outcome

CONFLICT RESOLUTION
- Mediation services: Available for workplace conflicts
- HR consultation: Guidance on resolving issues
- Manager support: Training on conflict management
- Peer mediation: For team-level disputes
- External resources: EAP counselors available

EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM
- Free counseling: 6 sessions per issue per year
- Available 24/7: Phone support hotline
- Confidential services: Privacy guaranteed
- Family support: Services extend to household members
- Resource referrals: Legal, financial, childcare support

WORKPLACE SAFETY
- Safety committee: Employee representatives
- Incident reporting: Required within 24 hours
- Safety training: Mandatory for all employees
- Emergency procedures: Posted and practiced
- Safety equipment: Provided and maintained

HEALTH AND WELLNESS
- Wellness program: Health screenings and challenges
- Mental health support: Resources and counseling
- Ergonomics: Workstation assessments available
- Fitness facilities: On-site gym access
- Healthy snacks: Provided in break rooms

COMMUNICATION CHANNELS
- All-hands meetings: Monthly company updates
- Department meetings: Weekly team updates
- Suggestion box: Anonymous feedback welcome
- Employee surveys: Annual engagement survey
- Open door policy: Access to leadership
