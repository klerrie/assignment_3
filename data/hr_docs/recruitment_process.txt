RECRUITMENT AND HIRING PROCESS

Our recruitment process ensures we find the best talent for our organization.

JOB POSTING
- All positions posted internally for 5 business days first
- External postings on company website and job boards
- Job descriptions must include salary range
- Postings remain active for minimum 30 days

APPLICATION REVIEW
- Applications reviewed within 48 hours of submission
- Initial screening based on qualifications and experience
- Automated system filters applications
- HR team conducts first round of reviews

INTERVIEW PROCESS
- Phone screening: 30 minutes with HR representative
- Technical assessment: Role-specific skills evaluation
- Panel interview: 2-3 team members, 60 minutes
- Final interview: Department head, 45 minutes
- Reference checks conducted after final interview

OFFER PROCESS
- Verbal offer extended within 3 business days
- Written offer letter sent within 24 hours
- Offer includes: salary, benefits, start date, reporting structure
- Candidates have 5 business days to respond
- Background check required before start date

ONBOARDING
- First day orientation: Company overview and policies
- Week 1: Department introduction and team meetings
- Month 1: Training program and goal setting
- 90-day check-in: Performance review and feedback

DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION
- Equal opportunity employer committed to diversity
- Unconscious bias training for all interviewers
- Diverse interview panels required
- Accommodations available for candidates with disabilities
