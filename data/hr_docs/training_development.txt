TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS

We invest in our employees' growth through comprehensive training programs.

ONBOARDING TRAINING
- New employee orientation: Full day program
- Company culture and values workshop
- Systems and tools training: 2 days
- Department-specific training: 1 week
- Buddy system: Assigned mentor for first 90 days

MANDATORY TRAINING
- Safety training: Annual requirement
- Harassment prevention: Every 2 years
- Cybersecurity awareness: Quarterly updates
- Code of conduct: Annual certification
- Data privacy training: Required for all employees

SKILL DEVELOPMENT
- Technical skills workshops: Monthly offerings
- Soft skills training: Communication, leadership, teamwork
- Certification support: Exam fees and study materials
- Online learning platform: Access to 10,000+ courses
- Internal training sessions: Led by subject matter experts

LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT
- Emerging leaders program: For high-potential employees
- Management training: Required before promotion
- Executive coaching: Available for senior roles
- Mentorship program: Cross-functional pairings
- Leadership book club: Monthly discussions

CAREER PLANNING
- Individual development plans: Created with manager
- Career pathing: Clear progression routes
- Internal job postings: Priority for current employees
- Lateral moves: Encouraged for skill building
- Promotion criteria: Transparent and documented

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT BUDGET
- Annual allocation: $2,000 per employee
- Can be used for: Conferences, courses, certifications, books
- Approval process: Manager approval required
- Unused budget: Does not roll over
- Additional budget: Available for critical skills
