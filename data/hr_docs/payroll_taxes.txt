PAYROLL AND TAX INFORMATION

Understanding your pay, deductions, and tax obligations.

PAY SCHEDULE
- Pay frequency: Bi-weekly on Fridays
- Direct deposit: Required, set up in first week
- Pay stubs: Available online portal
- Overtime: Paid at 1.5x hourly rate
- Holiday pay: Time and a half for worked holidays

PAYROLL DEDUCTIONS
- Federal taxes: Withheld based on W-4
- State taxes: Varies by state of residence
- Social Security: 6.2% up to wage base
- Medicare: 1.45% of all wages
- Health insurance: Employee portion
- 401(k): Employee contribution percentage

TAX FORMS
- W-2: Issued by January 31
- 1099: For contractors, by January 31
- W-4: Update through HR portal
- State forms: Provided as required
- Tax resources: HR can provide guidance

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
- Submission: Within 30 days of expense
- Approval: Manager approval required
- Processing: 5-7 business days
- Receipts: Required for all expenses
- Policy: Company expense policy applies

TIME TRACKING
- Hourly employees: Must log all hours
- Overtime: Pre-approval required
- PTO requests: Submit through system
- Timesheet: Due by Friday 5 PM
- Corrections: Contact payroll within 2 weeks

PAYROLL QUESTIONS
- Contact: payroll@company.com
- Response time: Within 24 hours
- Portal: Self-service for most needs
- Direct deposit: Update through portal
- Tax changes: Update W-4 as needed
