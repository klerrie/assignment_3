PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Our performance management system ensures continuous feedback and development.

GOAL SETTING
- Annual goals set in January with manager
- SMART goals: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
- Individual goals aligned with department objectives
- Quarterly goal reviews and adjustments
- Goals documented in performance management system

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
- Annual review: Comprehensive evaluation in December
- Self-assessment: Employee completes before review
- Manager assessment: Based on goals and observations
- 360-degree feedback: For leadership roles
- Review discussion: 60-minute meeting with manager

PERFORMANCE RATINGS
- Exceeds expectations: Top 10% of performers
- Meets expectations: Solid performance, most employees
- Needs improvement: Below expectations, development plan
- Unsatisfactory: Performance issues, formal action
- Ratings impact: Bonuses, promotions, development

FEEDBACK CULTURE
- Regular one-on-ones: Weekly with direct manager
- Real-time feedback: Encouraged throughout year
- Peer feedback: Available through system
- 360 reviews: Annual for managers and above
- Feedback training: Provided to all managers

PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT
- Performance improvement plan: 30-90 day program
- Clear expectations: Documented goals and metrics
- Regular check-ins: Weekly progress reviews
- Support provided: Training and resources
- Outcomes: Improvement, role change, or separation

RECOGNITION PROGRAMS
- Employee of the month: Department nominations
- Spot awards: Immediate recognition for achievements
- Years of service: Annual recognition ceremony
- Peer recognition: Public appreciation platform
- Annual awards: Excellence in various categories
