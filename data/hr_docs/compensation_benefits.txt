COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS GUIDE

This document outlines our comprehensive compensation and benefits package.

SALARY STRUCTURE
- Base salary determined by role, experience, and market rates
- Annual salary reviews conducted in Q4
- Merit increases based on performance ratings
- Market adjustments reviewed annually
- Salary bands published internally for transparency

BONUS STRUCTURE
- Annual performance bonus: 0-20% of base salary
- Bonus calculation based on individual and company performance
- Discretionary bonuses for exceptional contributions
- Spot bonuses for outstanding achievements
- Bonus payments distributed in March

EQUITY COMPENSATION
- Stock options granted to eligible employees
- Vesting schedule: 25% after year 1, then monthly
- 4-year total vesting period
- Exercise period: 10 years from grant date
- Equity refreshers for top performers

HEALTH INSURANCE
- Medical: PPO and HMO options available
- Company covers 80% of premium costs
- Deductible: $1,500 individual, $3,000 family
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $5,000 individual
- Preventive care covered at 100%

RETIREMENT BENEFITS
- 401(k) plan with immediate eligibility
- Company match: 100% of first 3%, 50% of next 3%
- Maximum match: 4.5% of salary
- Vesting: Immediate for employee contributions
- Company contributions vest over 3 years

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS
- Gym membership reimbursement: $50/month
- Commuter benefits: Pre-tax transit passes
- Employee assistance program: Free counseling services
- Tuition reimbursement: Up to $5,000 per year
- Wellness programs: Health screenings and workshops
