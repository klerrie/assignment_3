EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

Guidelines for submitting and getting reimbursed for business expenses.

ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
- Travel: Flights, hotels, rental cars, meals
- Meals: Business meals with clients or team
- Transportation: Taxis, rideshare, parking, tolls
- Supplies: Office supplies, equipment under $500
- Professional development: Conferences, training, courses
- Client entertainment: Meals, events, gifts (with limits)

EXPENSE LIMITS
- Meals: $75 per person per day
- Hotels: $200 per night (higher in major cities)
- Flights: Economy class, business with approval
- Ground transportation: Reasonable and necessary
- Client gifts: $100 maximum per recipient
- Entertainment: Pre-approval required over $500

SUBMISSION PROCESS
- Timeline: Submit within 30 days of expense
- System: Use expense management portal
- Receipts: Required for all expenses over $25
- Approval: Manager approval required
- Processing: 5-7 business days after approval
- Payment: Direct deposit to bank account

RECEIPT REQUIREMENTS
- Format: Original receipts, photos, or PDFs
- Details: Date, amount, vendor, description
- Missing receipts: Explanation required
- Credit card statements: Not sufficient alone
- Foreign currency: Convert to USD with date

NON-REIMBURSABLE
- Personal expenses: Not business-related
- Alcohol: Not reimbursable (except client entertainment)
- Fines: Traffic tickets, parking violations
- Personal items: Clothing, toiletries
- Spouse/guest: Travel for non-employees
- Insurance: Personal travel insurance

TRAVEL EXPENSES
- Airfare: Book through company travel portal
- Hotels: Preferred hotels for discounts
- Meals: Per diem or actual expenses
- Incidentals: Tips, baggage fees
- Advance: Can request travel advance
- Policy: Follow company travel policy

APPROVAL WORKFLOW
- Amount: Under $100, manager approval
- Amount: $100-$1000, department head
- Amount: Over $1000, VP approval
- Urgent: Expedited approval available
- Denied: Can appeal with additional documentation
