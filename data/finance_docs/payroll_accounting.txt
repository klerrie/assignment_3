PAYROLL ACCOUNTING AND PROCESSING

Accounting for payroll and employee compensation.

PAYROLL COMPONENTS
- Gross pay: Base salary or hourly wages
- Deductions: Taxes, benefits, 401(k)
- Net pay: Amount paid to employee
- Employer taxes: FICA, unemployment
- Benefits: Employer portion of benefits
- Accruals: Vacation and sick time

PAYROLL PROCESSING
- Frequency: Bi-weekly payroll processing
- Timeline: Process 2 days before pay date
- System: Automated payroll system
- Validation: Validate before processing
- Approval: Finance approval required
- Distribution: Direct deposit or checks

PAYROLL JOURNAL ENTRIES
- Wages: Debit wage expense
- Taxes: Credit tax liabilities
- Benefits: Credit benefit liabilities
- Cash: Credit cash for net pay
- Accruals: Accrue payroll if period end
- Reversals: Reverse accruals when paid

PAYROLL TAXES
- Federal: Federal income tax withholding
- FICA: Social Security and Medicare
- State: State income tax withholding
- Local: Local tax withholding
- Unemployment: Federal and state unemployment
- Deposits: Tax deposit schedule

BENEFIT ACCOUNTING
- Health insurance: Employer portion expense
- 401(k) match: Employer contribution expense
- Other benefits: Life, disability, etc.
- Accrual: Accrue benefits monthly
- Payment: Pay when due
- Reconciliation: Reconcile benefit accounts

VACATION AND SICK TIME
- Accrual: Accrue based on policy
- Rate: Accrual rate per pay period
- Liability: Track as liability
- Usage: Reduce liability when used
- Cap: Maximum accrual limits
- Payout: Account for payouts on termination

PAYROLL RECONCILIATION
- Process: Monthly reconciliation
- Components: Wages, taxes, benefits
- Variance: Investigate variances
- Adjustments: Make adjusting entries
- Documentation: Document reconciliation
- Review: Management review

PAYROLL REPORTING
- Reports: Various payroll reports
- Tax forms: W-2, 941, state forms
- Benefits: Benefit reports
- Analysis: Payroll analysis
- Budget: Payroll budget reports
- Compliance: Compliance reporting

PAYROLL CONTROLS
- Authorization: Authorize payroll changes
- Segregation: Segregate duties
- Review: Review payroll before processing
- Approval: Approve payroll
- Reconciliation: Regular reconciliations
- Monitoring: Ongoing monitoring
