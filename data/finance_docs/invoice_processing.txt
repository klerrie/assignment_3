INVOICE PROCESSING AND PAYABLES

How to submit invoices and process vendor payments.

INVOICE SUBMISSION
- Portal: Submit through accounts payable portal
- Format: PDF preferred, images accepted
- Details: Vendor, amount, date, description
- Approval: Manager approval required
- Attachments: Purchase orders, contracts
- Deadline: Submit by 25th for next month payment

INVOICE REQUIREMENTS
- Information: Vendor name, address, tax ID
- Details: Line items with descriptions
- Amount: Total amount clearly stated
- Terms: Payment terms (Net 30, etc.)
- Date: Invoice date and due date
- PO: Reference purchase order if applicable

APPROVAL PROCESS
- Amount: Under $1,000, manager approval
- Amount: $1,000-$10,000, department head
- Amount: Over $10,000, VP or CFO
- Routing: Automatic routing to approvers
- Timeline: 3 business days for approval
- Escalation: Auto-escalates if not approved

PAYMENT SCHEDULE
- Cycle: Payments processed twice monthly
- Dates: 5th and 20th of each month
- Method: ACH or check based on vendor
- Terms: Pay according to invoice terms
- Early payment: Discounts available sometimes
- Late payment: Avoid late fees

VENDOR SETUP
- New vendor: Complete vendor setup form
- Information: W-9, banking details, contact
- Verification: Finance verifies information
- Approval: Finance and procurement approve
- System: Added to vendor master file
- Payment: Can process payments after setup

PURCHASE ORDERS
- Required: PO required for purchases over $500
- Process: Create PO in procurement system
- Approval: Based on amount thresholds
- Receiving: Match invoice to PO
- Three-way match: PO, receipt, invoice
- Exceptions: Resolve mismatches before payment

DISPUTES AND ISSUES
- Discrepancies: Contact vendor to resolve
- Credits: Process credit memos for returns
- Adjustments: Submit adjustment requests
- Documentation: Keep all correspondence
- Escalation: Escalate if vendor unresponsive
- Resolution: Document resolution in system

PAYMENT METHODS
- ACH: Electronic transfer, preferred method
- Check: Mailed checks for some vendors
- Wire: International or urgent payments
- Credit card: For small purchases
- Timing: 2-3 business days for ACH
- Confirmation: Payment confirmations sent
