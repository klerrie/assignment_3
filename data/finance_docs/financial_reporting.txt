FINANCIAL REPORTING AND ANALYSIS

Understanding financial reports and key metrics.

MONTHLY REPORTS
- P&L: Profit and loss statement
- Balance sheet: Assets, liabilities, equity
- Cash flow: Operating, investing, financing
- Department: Department-level P&L
- Variance: Budget vs actual analysis
- Distribution: Sent by 10th of following month

KEY METRICS
- Revenue: Total sales and revenue
- Expenses: Operating expenses by category
- EBITDA: Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation
- Cash position: Available cash and equivalents
- Burn rate: Monthly cash consumption
- Runway: Months of cash remaining

DEPARTMENT REPORTS
- Revenue: Department revenue contribution
- Expenses: Department spending by category
- Budget: Budget vs actual by department
- Headcount: FTE and contractor costs
- Trends: Month-over-month and year-over-year
- Forecast: Updated forecasts quarterly

ANNUAL REPORTS
- Financial statements: Audited annual statements
- Management discussion: MD&A section
- Board presentation: Executive summary
- Tax returns: Filed by tax deadline
- Compliance: Regulatory filings as required
- Distribution: Shared with stakeholders

FORECASTING
- Process: Quarterly forecasting process
- Horizon: 12-month rolling forecast
- Updates: Updated monthly with actuals
- Drivers: Key business drivers identified
- Scenarios: Best case, base case, worst case
- Accuracy: Track forecast accuracy

DASHBOARDS
- Executive: High-level KPIs and metrics
- Department: Department-specific dashboards
- Real-time: Live data where available
- Custom: Can request custom dashboards
- Access: Role-based access control
- Training: Dashboard training available

ANALYSIS TOOLS
- Excel: Standard analysis tool
- BI tools: Tableau, Power BI available
- ERP: Financial data in ERP system
- Exports: Data exports available
- Support: Finance team provides support
- Training: Analysis training offered

COMPLIANCE REPORTING
- Tax: Quarterly and annual tax reports
- Regulatory: Industry-specific reports
- Audit: Support for external audits
- Internal audit: Internal audit support
- Documentation: Maintain audit trail
- Deadlines: Meet all reporting deadlines
