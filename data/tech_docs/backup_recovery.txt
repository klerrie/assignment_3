BACKUP AND DISASTER RECOVERY PROCEDURES

How we protect and recover your data and systems.

BACKUP SCHEDULE
- Files: Daily incremental backups
- Full backup: Weekly on Sundays
- Databases: Every 6 hours
- Email: Continuous backup
- Retention: 30 days for files, 1 year for critical data

BACKUP LOCATIONS
- Primary: On-premises backup server
- Secondary: Cloud backup to AWS
- Geographic: Backup in different region
- Redundancy: Multiple copies maintained
- Encryption: All backups encrypted

FILE RECOVERY
- Self-service: Restore files from OneDrive
- Version history: 30 days of versions
- Deleted files: Recoverable for 30 days
- Request: Submit ticket for older recoveries
- Process: Usually completed within 4 hours

SYSTEM RECOVERY
- RTO: Recovery time objective of 4 hours
- RPO: Recovery point objective of 1 hour
- Testing: Quarterly disaster recovery tests
- Documentation: Detailed recovery procedures
- Team: Dedicated DR team on call

DATA RETENTION
- Active files: Indefinite while employed
- Deleted files: 30 days in recycle bin
- Archived: Moved to archive after 1 year
- Legal hold: Retained per legal requirements
- Purging: Automatic after retention period

CLOUD BACKUP
- OneDrive: Automatic sync and backup
- SharePoint: Version history maintained
- Teams: Chat and files backed up
- Recovery: Self-service restore available
- Support: IT can assist with recovery

DISASTER RECOVERY PLAN
- Scenarios: Documented for various disasters
- Roles: Assigned recovery team members
- Communication: Notification procedures
- Testing: Annual full DR test
- Updates: Plan reviewed quarterly
